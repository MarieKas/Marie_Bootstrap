About Me

I am a Web Developer and Designer in London. I enjoy designing websites and animations on websites and other projects. I am a well-mannered, hardworking and very enthusiastic individual with a passion for building new things. I have spent the last four years serving within the Ministry of Justice, having undertaken different responsibilities in various entities.

I have acquired indispensable skills such as using different technologies deployed at the MOJ, and developed a knack for problem-solving in a fast-paced environment. I am disciplined and very professional, which is mandatory within the technology industry. I am very ambitious and endeavor to further my career by undertaking any training necessary in order to become the best I can be, and deliver the best service possible.
